Tales of Mourer: The Devil's Pass

A fisherman lost in his own fears and past is drawn into the war of the universe.

Despite the war, Mourer hides by fishing. He wants to move on, to have love and children, to trust. But the world doesn't want him. At least not until they think he can help them steal the treasure they need to defeat the Elishians. A treasure that was supposedly lost in a place no one believes is real. But this treasure holds more secrets and trouble than what he bargained for. The Tales of Mourer is an adventure of finding one's people and gaining confidence.

Imaginings of the Beyond

Imaginings of the Beyond is a collection of 9 short stories that delves into the realms of fantasy and magic. This collection features tales of enchanted worlds, mythical creatures, and extraordinary powers, where the boundaries of reality blur and the extraordinary  become possible. Each story invites readers to explore new dimensions and immerse themselves in imaginative landscapes filled with wonder and intrigue.

The Light and The Dark

When a young man follows his God's direction to protect the people of his island, he is thrown into turmoil. Wren's people have been holding onto traditions the people of the Sea Serpents insist should be destroyed. When his town is burned to the ground, will he be able to protect those who follow him? And will what is buried beneath the island help or destroy them? Can he follow his God's plan?

The Light and The Dark is a story of loss, fear, and peace where dragons, krakens, and phoenixes come alive.
